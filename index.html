How to Play

  • Enter your name and choose a word blend category (e.g. CH, DR, SH...).
  • Pick a difficulty: Easy plays audio AND shows a word definition hint; Hard plays audio only — no hints!
  • Tap 🔊 to hear the word again. Type the spelling and press Submit or Enter.
  • Correct answers give points and fun animations. Your results are saved locally.
